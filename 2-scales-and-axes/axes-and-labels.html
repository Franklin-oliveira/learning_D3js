Adding Axes and Labels

Now it's time for us to add axes and labels to our simple bar chart. But first, we need to create SVG's margins and groups to create space for us to insert our axes and be able to move the whole figure (all elements at the same time).

Groups: are invisible containers for structuring SVG. It has translation attribute to move multiple SVGs at once.

To create an axis, we can use a D3 axis generator (one of axisLeft, axisTop, axisBottom and axisRight) and append it to our svg group, with a class attribute.

See ./js/axes-and-labels.js for details.
